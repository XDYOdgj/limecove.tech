Enhance Your Kitchen Safety with PATIKIL Heat Shrink Sleeves

PATIKIL provides high-quality, non-slip heat shrink sleeves designed to keep your kitchen knives safe, organized, and protected. The durable PE material ensures a firm grip, reducing the risk of accidental slipping during use. With a yellow color option, these sleeves offer a visual indicator to help you distinguish between knives used for raw meat, vegetables, fruits, or cooked foods, making food safety easier to manage.

Why Choose PATIKIL Heat Shrink Sleeves?

  • Non-slip PE material for secure grip and protection against moisture and dirt
  • Quick shrinkage with heat for easy installation using a hot air machine
  • Yellow color coding enhances organization and helps with hygiene tracking
  • Stable and durable for long-lasting use in your kitchen toolkit
  • Perfect fit for standard cutting knife handles, ensuring a snug and secure wrap

Each pack includes 4 sleeves, sized at 55x125mm, making them ideal for everyday use in both professional and home kitchen settings. These sleeves are not only practical but also add a touch of style to your kitchen tools while ensuring better hygiene and safety.
