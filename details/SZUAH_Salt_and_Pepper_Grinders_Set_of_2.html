SZUAH Salt and Pepper Grinders Set of 2

Elevate your cooking with this elegant and functional set of two grinders, perfect for home chefs and food lovers. Premium Craftsmanship

Each grinder is crafted from premium natural oak with a sturdy stainless steel and aluminum construction, ensuring long-lasting durability and a timeless aesthetic. Their sleek design not only looks great but also fits seamlessly into modern and traditional kitchen setups alike.

The adjustable nut allows you to control the grind from coarse to fine, giving you complete control over the texture of your spices. Whether you're grinding fresh peppercorns or simply seasoning your dish, this feature adapts to your needs and preferences with ease.

Moisture-Resistant Design

The wooden top cap helps keep your spices dry and fresh, ensuring optimal flavor every time you use the grinder. This design is especially beneficial for those who prefer to store their spices for extended periods without worry of spoilage.

Convenience and Style

The set includes a handy cleaning brush and a wooden stand for effortless storage and maintenance. These small but smart additions make the grinders not only functional but also a pleasure to use and keep organized.

Color-Coded for Easy Use

The two grinders come in different colors, making it easy to tell which one is for salt and which is for pepper, or even for other spices if you're looking to expand your collection. This added detail is perfect for both home and professional kitchens.

Compact and Versatile

With a compact size of 8'' X 2.1'', these grinders are easy to place on any kitchen counter, table, or island. Their interchangeable design means they can be used for various spices, giving you more flexibility in your culinary routine.
